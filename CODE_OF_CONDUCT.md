# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a welcoming, safe, and
equitable experience for everyone.

## Encouraged Behaviors

1. Respecting the **purpose of our community** and our ways of gathering.
2. Engaging **kindly and honestly** with others.
3. Respecting **different viewpoints** and experiences.
4. **Taking responsibility** for our actions and contributions.
5. Gracefully giving and accepting **constructive feedback**.
6. Committing to **repairing harm** when it occurs.

## Restricted Behaviors

1. **Harassment.** Violating expressed boundaries or engaging in unwelcome personal attention.
2. **Character attacks.** Insulting, demeaning, or pejorative comments directed at community members.
3. **Stereotyping or discrimination.** Characterizing behavior based on immutable identities or traits.
4. **Sexualization.** Behavior considered inappropriately intimate in the context of this community.
5. **Violating confidentiality.** Sharing someone's personal information without their permission.
6. **Endangerment.** Causing, encouraging, or threatening harm toward any person.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to
**[contact@writronic.com](mailto:contact@writronic.com)**.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with confidentiality.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and when representing
the project in public spaces.
